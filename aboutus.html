About Tesla

Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk, Martin Eberhard, Marc Tarpenning, JB Straubel, and Ian Wright. The company specializes in electric car manufacturing, battery energy storage, and renewable energy solutions.

Our Mission

At Tesla, our mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. We believe in creating innovative, high-quality products that not only provide exceptional performance but also contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable future.

Technology and Innovation

Tesla is known for its cutting-edge technology and continuous innovation. From electric vehicles with Autopilot capabilities to energy products like solar panels and Powerwall, we strive to push the boundaries of what is possible in the pursuit of a better world.
